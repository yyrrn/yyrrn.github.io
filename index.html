New Dress To Impress Update(February 2025)


We have new update in DTI! The Valentine's Day Update for Dress to Impress (DTI) is set to release on February 8, 2025, and will be available until March 8, 2025. This update brings exciting new features, clothing items, poses, and more to enhance the gameplay experience.

New Gameplay Features

Duo Poses: A highly anticipated feature where models can interact in paired animations, such as one carrying Seasonal Item Chests: To improve game organization and performance, seasonal items will now be stored in special chests around the map. Model Enhancements: Models will now have visible ears without needing earrings, and new hairstyles will be available. Expanded Patterns & Textures: Players can now choose from new pattern categories like floral, geometric, and metallic designs to customize outfits.




New Fashion Items & Accessories


This update introduces a brand-new sleeveless dress featuring pink metal chain straps, a heart corsage, draped layers, and a lace-up corset. The popular Granny Dress has also been redesigned, offering toggle options for different top and skirt combinations. For male avatars, there are new clothing and accessory options, allowing for greater customization and style variety.



Additional Updates & Features

Redeemable codes for exclusive in-game items
A redesigned runway for a fresh new look
New background music and effects
A new chapter in the Lana Lore series
