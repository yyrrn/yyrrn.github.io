What Are Codes in Dress to Impress?

In Roblox games, codes are special promo words or letter combinations that developers release. When you enter these codes in the game, you can get free items, such as:

  • Exclusive clothing (dresses, shirts, skirts, etc.)
  • Accessories (bags, hats, bows, etc.)
  • Special hairstyles
  • Other cosmetic items
